1 Overview

Obtaining and synthesizing the data may be complicated, but the actual calculation of fisheries status is actually simple. At its core, fisheries status is the average of the stock status scores (e.g., B/Bmsy) weighted by the stocks catch (e.g., tonnes) within a region.
